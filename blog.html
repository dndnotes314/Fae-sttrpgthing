Hello Adventurers!

Neverwinter held by the crown king Dagult Neverember. A diverse city filled by all manner of races and unique foods through out the streets. You are all adventurers of the guild hall Call of Fates. There you met Viktor a well dressed half-elven man with slicked back parted hair. His cologne is strong, it smells of apple wood and gunmetal. He tells you to retrieve an item known as the Obsidian Egg, before a cult gets their hands on the egg. So you a group of adventures decide to head to Dragon's Harp or also know as Stormwreck Isle. It got the nickname Stormwreck because of boats have been known to go missing around the isle.
